# Volumetric Concrete Equations

Concrete is ordered and estimated in cubic meters (\(m^3\)). The basic equation is:

$$V_{\text{net}} = L \times W \times H$$

To account for standard structural wastes, we introduce the wastage factor (\(W_f\)):

$$V_{\text{gross}} = V_{\text{net}} \times (1 + W_f)$$

### Standard Wastage Values
- Beams & Columns: **5% (0.05)** wastage factor.
- Foundation Piles: **10% (0.10)** wastage factor.
- Thin Floor Slabs: **8% (0.08)** wastage factor.
